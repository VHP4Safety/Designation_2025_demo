Q S P R P R E D


QSPRpred is open-source software libary for building Quantitative Structure Property/Activity Relationship (QSPR/QSAR) models. It provides a unified interface for building QSPR models based on different types of descriptors and machine learning algorithms.

QSPRpred offers functionality to design custom models or use pretrained QSAR models. Pretrained models that are provided by VHP4Safety are found under 'VHP4Safety Models' in the manual section.
